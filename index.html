Fat Sunday

ABOUT US

Nothing brings people together quite like food. Whether it be the family around the dinner table, or friends out on the town for a social evening. Fat Sunday was created with just that in mind. It all started with Frank Cunningham, a guy who just wanted to cook for his friends while they watched sports on Sunday. After many years of doing this, many began to joke, “the day we eat like fat kids.” From then on it was known and referred to as Fat Sunday. Every Sunday, friends gathered around for sports and food. It brought everyone together and was something to look forward to, inspiring Frank to take things to the next level. This meant bigger dinner parties, catering events, and continuing his education to hone and perfect his art. During that time Frank partnered with Erica Coffee. Together they were able to take that idea and turn it into a dream come true. Fat Sunday now not only offers catering, but they also offer: meal plans, personal chef services, and so much more! Whether you have parties to feed, or just yourself, we at Fat Sunday are here to help.
